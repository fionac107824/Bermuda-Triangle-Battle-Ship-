Intital Designs

Here are some of our intial ideas when starting are game designing process:

  • Forest Fire Pygame:

    • This game was supposed to be designed through the Pygame software. Originally, we wanted this to be based off of the Happy Garden game in the coding games in python book. There a cow in a forest. Then fires are spreading everywhere. The cow is the person trying to put out the fire, basically like a firefighter.Then trees are placed everywhere and the cow has to stop the forest fire. Though we liked the idea, we realized that actually implementing the game would pose many difficulties as our game would be controlled by the cricket board. We eventually realized that the complexity of the game's design would not be plausible game idea.

  • Animal Crossing Board Game:

    • This game was a board game that we designed tlizing small figurines of animals. There were four animals that were supposed to get across to cross diagonally over the forest to their new home. Then, on the different blacks they would have colors indicating a teleportation.

  • Realistic Monopoly:

    • We originally wanted to make a game based off of monopoly. We wanted to give it more of a tech category by assigning one of the big four tech companies from Apple,Samsung, Microsoft, and Google and then have each player play as that company. Each time that a company goes to jail, they lose money. In the end, we realized that we could not attribute a greater significance to the game thus changing our idea. From this design, we learned that while game design is a creativeprocess, we also need a greater purpose for our game to make it entertaining.
